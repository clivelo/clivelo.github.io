Hey, I'm Clive—an AI enthusiast with a Psychology background from Hong Kong. I majored in Psychology as I loved (and still love!) studying human intentions, cognition, and behaviors. I did my research master's degree in Psychology with a specific focus on cognitive neuroscience. My brain was trying to learn about itself, which is both terrifying and fascinating! My thesis was about how individual differences in affective touch preference are manifested in the brain by measuring people’s brain activity using an electroencephalogram.

I would say my passion for AI started from programming. My coding journey began in high school. Started with Free Pascal, then moved on to Java. I still remember the Sudoku project I wrote in Java for my final-year programming homework back then, it was pretty horrible not gonna lie. But for the next few years, I did a myriad of personal projects in Java in my spare time and it quickly became my hobby. Eventually, I got introduced to Python when I read about how user-friendly the language and how open the community is, I’ve been hooked ever since. I started with scikit-learn and messed around with SVM and clustering. The open-source community allowed me to explore different datasets. Then, with TensorFlow and p5.js, I made some fun and creative projects that I’m really proud of.

My goal is to integrate AI with human psychology and to use neural networks to model the human brain.
